### Law of Total Probability

Let $A_1, ... , A_n $ be a **partition** of $B$, then
$$P(B) = P(B \cap A_1) + ... + P(B \cap A_n) \newline = P(B|A_1)P(A_1) + ... + P(B|A_n)P(A_n)  $$
